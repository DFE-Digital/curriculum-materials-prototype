Manage key stage 2 and 3 curriculum resources

This service is for teachers whose school has joined the Department for Education's curriculum materials programme.

Use this service to:

  • manage the content of your units and lessons
  • find new teaching materials that fit your school's curriculum
  • review your lessons' content to ensure teaching is in sequence

Before you start

Your school can join the curriculum materials programme if it is:

  • maintained or local authority funded
  • an academy or academy trust
  • a free school

The online service is only available in England.
